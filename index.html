Interests

I'm particularly interested in the intersection of high-performance computing and AI. I've always been drawn to GPUs, I like staying up to date with the newest innovations in that space and digging into the specs of the latest hardware. Most people hear “GPU” and think strictly of gaming, as I once did, but it's amazing how powerful and efficient they are for accelerating workloads like machine learning and computer vision, and for enabling high-throughput, multithreaded, parallel processing.

A big focus for me right now is generative AI. I'm interested in how models can be integrated into real products that simplify workflows for non-technical users and provide tools that assist developers. I am currently completing a generative AI course on DeepLearning.AI that covers everything from prompt engineering to efficiently training models using fine-tuning methods like LoRA.

Overall, my interests revolve around studying and keeping up to date with technologies that use GPUs and AI in a way that’s relevant and useful.

Outside of tech, I enjoy reading books on different theologies, athletic training, and fishing.
